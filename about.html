Who I Am

Computer Engineer, AI researcher, and community builder from Mizoram University.

I'm Ekjot Singh Nagpal, a Computer Engineering student (2023–2027) at Mizoram University, Aizawl. My work sits at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and real-world impact — with a particular focus on AI-driven healthcare applications.

I serve as AI Club Coordinator at Mizoram University, organising workshops, hackathons, and seminars to build a strong AI learning community on campus. Leading people and ideas energises me just as much as writing code.

As Technical Lead at Ayulex, a student startup recognised under MeitY's GENESIS EiR Cohort 2 and backed by TiLi Incubator with a ₹5 lakh grant, I lead AI/ML research end-to-end — bridging technical execution with strategic vision.

I'm also an NCC cadet, which has shaped my discipline, leadership, and teamwork in ways that complement everything I do. Beyond the screen, I sketch, trek, listen to soft music, and enjoy competitive e-gaming.
